About Dr.Arvind

Dr.Arvind Kumar with over 25 years of experience is a leading Laparoscopic and Oncosurgeon in North India practising at Sir Gangaram Hospital,New Delhi. Dr.Arvind is formally trained byDr. Shailesh Puntambekar who is one of the world’s renowned Laparoscopic Oncosurgeons. In addition to his clinical activities, Dr.Arvind is active in research & education and has been providing fellowship & mentorship to several surgeons from India as well as abroad. He has a rich clinical experience of more than 25 years and accomplishing more than 10,000 laparoscopic surgeries including GI and hepatobiliary Oncology, Gynae-oncology, Thoracic Oncology.
