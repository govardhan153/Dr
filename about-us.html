Dr.Arvind Kumar with over 25 years of experience is a leading Laparoscopic and Oncosurgeon in North India practising at Sir Gangaram Hospital,New Delhi. Dr.Arvind is formally trained byDr. Shailesh Puntambekar who is one of the world’s renowned Laparoscopic Oncosurgeons. In addition to his clinical activities, Dr.Arvind is active in research & education and has been providing fellowship & mentorship to several surgeons from India as well as abroad.

He has a rich clinical experience of more than 25 years and accomplishing more than 10,000 laparoscopic surgeries including GI and hepatobiliary Oncology, Gynae-oncology, Thoracic Oncology. Also, he is an international operating faculty at Tashkent.

Accomplishments Invited by Tashkent Medical Academy, Uzbekistan to deliver a master class and to perform advanced laparoscopic surgeries in a live operative workshop. Invited by Kazakhstan society of endoscopic surgeon in 2013 and 2014 to demonstrate live transthoracic esophagectomy and colorectal surgery. He has been invited as an operating faculty in various knowledge sharing national and international workshops & conferences like IAGES, SELSI, and GALAGES etc. He regularly conducts “Train the Trainer” programs and “Live Operative Laparoscopic Workshops” as National Faculty for Ethicon Institute of Surgical Education (EISE), Johnson & Johnson Ltd.
